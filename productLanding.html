About Us

Hermes Scooters was founded with the goal of making motorized travel accessible for city dwellers. We understand the need to have access to non car vehicles in cities like New York, Chicago and Seattle. It is our mission to create motorized travel equity within urban communities and foster a fair priced alternative to public transportation and personal cars.
